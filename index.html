About Finance Intelligence
What is this?
The Finance Intelligence Platform is a free AI-powered daily briefing tool built exclusively for finance and banking professionals in Indonesia. Every morning it generates fresh, rich intelligence on your chosen topic — tailored to your interests. Think of it as your personal Bloomberg Terminal meets a leadership coach, available in under 2 minutes every day.

How to use it
  • Enter your name and select your primary interest on first visit.
  • You land on the Home dashboard showing all 7 topic channels.
  • Click any topic card to open its focused intelligence briefing.
  • Each topic has a headline, detailed bullets, a leadership insight, and a Deep Dive for full analysis.
  • Use Jump to another topic links at the top of each page to switch between topics instantly.
  • Share any insight directly to WhatsApp or copy it for use in meetings.
  • Hit Refresh to generate a completely new briefing for that topic anytime.
